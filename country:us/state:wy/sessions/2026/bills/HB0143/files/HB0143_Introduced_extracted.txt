10 35-34-103. Free speech protected from state
11 discrimination; notice of speech-based complaints; civil
12 remedies.
13
14 (a) No state agency shall discriminate against a
15 health care provider for engaging in speech, expression or
16 association that occurs within the scope of providing
17 health care services or medical advice and that is
18 protected from government interference by the first
19 amendment to the United States constitution and article 1,
20 sections 20 and 21 of the Wyoming constitution, unless the
21 state agency demonstrates by clear and convincing evidence
22 that the health care provider's speech, expression or
23 association was the direct and proximate cause of physical
