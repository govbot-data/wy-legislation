2 39-15-603. Legislative intent.
3
4 (a) It is the intent of the legislature that the
5 funds distributed under this article shall not be used for:
6
7 (i) Salary adjustments, additional personnel or
8 increased personnel benefits;
9
10 (ii) Any compensation to the members of any
11 board for which the board of county commissioners appoints
12 members, unless compensation is otherwise required by law.
