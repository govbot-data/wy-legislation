20 22-6-201. Ballot security, custody, transport and storage.
21
22 (a) The secretary of state shall adopt uniform statewide
23 standards for the secure production, transport, storage and
24 accounting of ballots that are consistent with the provisions of
25 this article without the elimination of voting machines.

27 (b) Each county clerk shall maintain a documented chain of
28 custody for all ballots and election materials from printing or
29 receipt of the ballots through final storage of the ballots after

34 and the seal or container number, if applicable.
