16 (a) Notwithstanding any other provision of law, each county

18 ballots for all in-person voting in all elections in Wyoming. The
19 county clerk shall provide all voters with a paper ballot and a

21
22 (b) Subsection (a) of this section shall not prohibit any
23 county clerk from providing electronic ballot marking devices for

25 devices shall comply with all federal accessibility requirements,

28 voting machines and electronic voting systems. Nothing in this
29 subsection shall authorize any county to use electronic ballot
30 marking devices as the default method of marking ballots in the
31 county.
32
33 (c) As used in this section:
34
35 (i) "Disability" has the meaning stated in the
36 Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990, as amended by the
37 Americans with Disabilities Amendments Act of 2008;
38
39 (ii) "Electronic ballot marking device" means any
40 electronic device that assists voters in marking ballots,
41 including, without limitation, any voting machine that uses a touch
42 screen to produce a marked paper ballot or ballot card.".
