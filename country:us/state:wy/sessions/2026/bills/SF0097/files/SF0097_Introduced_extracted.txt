1 (a) In addition to other forms of payment accepted at
2 the discretion of the government entity, a government
3 entity shall accept cash for any in-person transaction as a
4 form of payment for:
5
6 (i) Any fee, fine, tax or charge imposed by a
7 government entity;
8
9 (ii) Any payment made for or in relation to
10 admission to any event sponsored by the government entity.
11
12 (b) No government entity shall charge an additional
13 fee solely because a person made a cash payment. This
14 subsection shall not be construed to prohibit a government
15 entity from collecting fees otherwise authorized by law
16 regardless of payment method.
17
18 (c) For the purposes of this section, "government
19 entity" shall mean the state, its agencies, municipalities,
20 counties, school districts, public colleges and
21 universities, political subdivisions and special districts.

6 Section 2. Nothing in this act shall be construed to
