13 Section 1.
14
15 (a) The legislature finds that:
16
1 HB0055

2026 STATE OF WYOMING 26LSO-0325
1 (i) Wyoming faces housing supply constraints
2 that burden working families and impair economic growth;
3
4 (ii) The state holds nontrust, state-acquired
5 real property that is not necessary to an essential
6 governmental purpose;
7
8 (iii) Disposal of the nonessential real property
9 at nominal consideration to owner-occupant purchasers,
10 subject to strict covenants, clustering and reversion, will
11 promote broad-based homeownership while preventing
12 acquisition by business entities and large-scale
13 speculative buyers.
14
15 (b) It is the purpose of this act to create
16 owner-occupied homestead communities on eligible state
17 lands and to ensure the land is used for single-family
18 residential purposes. This act constitutes a valid public
19 purpose, of primary benefit to all citizens of the state of
20 Wyoming.
