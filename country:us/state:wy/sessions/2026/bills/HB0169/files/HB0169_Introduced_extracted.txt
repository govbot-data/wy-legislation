1 9-13-201. Prohibition on use of public funds for
2 certain organizations.
3
4 (a) As used in this section:
5
6 (i) "Public funds" means any funds appropriated
7 by the legislature or any money received by a state agency
8 or political subdivision pursuant to law;
9
10 (ii) "Specified organizations" means any of the
11 following:
12
13 (A) Environmental council of the states;
14
15 (B) Council of chief state school officers;
16
17 (C) National association of medicaid
18 directors;
19
20 (D) Association of state and territorial
21 health officials.

1 (b) No public funds shall be expended to pay
2 membership fees, dues or any other contribution to a
3 specified organization. For purposes of this section,
4 "contribution" shall include payments made in exchange for
5 membership benefits that do not include the purchase of
6 goods or services.
7
8 (c) No public funds shall be expended to pay for any
9 expenses where the primary purpose of the expenditure is to
10 participate in a specified organization. For purposes of
11 this section, "expenditure" shall include travel,
12 registration fees or other costs associated with
13 conferences, meetings or other events sponsored by a
14 specified organization.
15
16 Section 2. Nothing in this act shall be construed to
17 alter, amend or impair any contract or other agreement
18 entered into before the effective date of this act.
19
20 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2026.
