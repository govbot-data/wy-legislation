4 19-14-101. Burial of indigents.
5
6 (a) The board of county commissioners of each county
7 shall provide for the preparation of the body and
8 transmittal to and burial in the veteran's cemetery of any
9 veteran other than a dishonorably discharged veteran of the
10 armed forces of the United States who served on behalf of
11 the United States in any war or conflict as defined in
12 section 101, title 38, United States Code and who dies
13 leaving insufficient funds to defray the necessary funeral
14 expenses. Any veteran that qualifies under W.S. 42-2-103(c)
15 shall also qualify under this section. The amount expended
16 for transporting the body shall not exceed five hundred
17 dollars ($500.00). The amount paid under this section for
18 burial shall not exceed one thousand five hundred dollars
19 ($1,500.00). Each board shall account for the county
20 expenses incurred under this subsection during a fiscal
21 year. By August 1 of each year, a county may request
22 reimbursement by the department of family services for the
23 expenses incurred under this subsection for the preceding

4 (b) Before assuming charge and expense for
5 preparation and transmittal of the body and burial, each
6 board of county commissioners shall first determine
7 according to procedures they establish that the deceased
