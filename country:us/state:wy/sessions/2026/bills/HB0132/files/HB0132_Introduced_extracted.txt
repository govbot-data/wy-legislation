HOUSE BILL NO. HB0132
Nicotine products manufacturer licensing and reporting.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Davis, Clouston, Connolly
and Lawley and Senator(s) Jones and Schuler
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to nicotine products; requiring
2 manufacturers of nicotine products to obtain a license;
3 requiring manufacturers of electronic cigarettes and vapor
4 material to file certifications as specified; placing
5 restrictions on the sale and advertising of electronic
6 cigarettes and vapor material; requiring the department of
7 revenue to publish a directory of electronic cigarettes and
8 vapor material offered for sale in Wyoming by licensed
9 nicotine product manufacturers; prohibiting the sale of
10 nicotine products not included in the directory; requiring
11 biannual inspections of retailers, distributors and
12 wholesalers of electronic cigarettes and vapor material;
13 providing penalties; providing definitions; providing
14 rulemaking; making conforming amendments; and providing for
15 an effective date.
