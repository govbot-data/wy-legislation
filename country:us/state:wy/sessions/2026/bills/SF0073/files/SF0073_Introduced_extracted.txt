3 24-16-101. Definitions.
4
5 (a) As used in this article:
6
7 (i) "Commission" means the Wyoming
8 transportation commission;
9
10 (ii) "Department" means the department of
11 transportation;
12
13 (iii) "Highway" means a public road or street
14 now or subsequently designated as part of the state highway
15 system, including rights-of-way, bridges, tunnels, signs
16 and drainage infrastructure.
17
18 24-16-102. Highway tolling program; implementation;
19 requirements; report; rulemaking.
20
21 (a) The department shall have authority to create a
22 highway tolling program.
23

1 (b) The department may develop any documentation
2 required by federal law to implement tolling on a highway
3 including planning, financing, construction, operation,
4 maintenance and improvements. Once the department develops
5 the documentation required to toll a highway, the
6 department shall submit the documentation to the commission
7 and the federal highway administration for approval.
8
9 (c) Upon receiving approval from both the commission
10 and the federal highway administration, the department
11 shall, not later than thirty (30) days after receiving the
12 last approval, report to the joint transportation, highways
13 and military affairs interim committee.
14
15 (d) The commission and the department shall not
16 implement tolling on any highway unless the relevant
17 documentation has been approved pursuant to subsection (b)
18 of this section and the legislature enacts legislation
19 either granting general authority for the highway tolling
20 program to implement tolls on highways or authorizing
21 tolling for one (1) or more specific highways.
