7 "[STATE CAPITOL COMPLEX HOTEL AND CONFERENCE CENTER]
8
9 Section 328.
10
11 (a) The state building commission shall conduct a
12 feasibility study regarding constructing a hotel and conference
13 center in the vicinity of the Wyoming state capitol building,
14 preferably near or within the state capitol complex, the revenue
15 of which shall be deposited into the public school foundation
16 program account. The commission shall contact persons who are
17 interested in developing, constructing and operating the hotel and
18 conference center and shall solicit bids to determine the costs
19 involved with the project. The state building commission shall
20 coordinate with the state treasurer to determine the anticipated
21 annual revenue for the state that could result from the project.
22
23 (b) Not later than November 1, 2026, the state building
24 commission shall report to the joint appropriations committee and
25 the select committee on capital financing and investments
26 regarding the findings of the feasibility study required by
27 subsection (a) of this section.
28
29 (c) There is appropriated twenty-five thousand dollars
30 ($25,000.00) from the general fund to the state building commission
31 for purposes of conducting the feasibility study required by this
32 section. This appropriation shall not be transferred or expended
33 for any other purpose. It is the intent of the legislature that
34 this appropriation not be included in the state building commission
35 or state construction department's standard budget for the
36 immediately succeeding fiscal biennium.".
