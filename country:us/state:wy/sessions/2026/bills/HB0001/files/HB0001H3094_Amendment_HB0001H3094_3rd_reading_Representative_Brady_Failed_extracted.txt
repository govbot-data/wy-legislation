4 "[GOVERNMENT MEMBER ASSOCIATIONS]
5
6 Section 328.
7
8 (a) No funds appropriated under this act shall be expended by
9 any agency for any of the following:
10
11 (i) The payment of membership fees or dues to any
12 government member association;
13
14 (ii) Reimbursement for the expenses of room, board or
15 travel that are related to the agency's participation in the
16 activities of a government member association;
17
18 (iii) Payment by or on behalf of an agency to any
19 government member association for the purposes of lobbying
20 activities pursuant to W.S. 28-7-101;
21
22 (iv) Contributions to political campaigns or candidates.
23
24 (b) As used in this section, "government member association"
25 means any association or organization which is composed primarily
26 of an agency, city, town or county or their officers or employees
27 and whose purposes include influencing legislative or
28 administrative action. "Government member association" does not
29 include entities created through an interstate compact or joint
30 powers boards formed for the primary purpose of performing a
31 specific governmental function or service.".
