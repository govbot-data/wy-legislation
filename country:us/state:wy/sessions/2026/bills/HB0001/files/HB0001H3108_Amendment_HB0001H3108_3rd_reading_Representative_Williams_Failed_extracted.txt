7 "[WYOMING GENERATIONAL INVESTMENT ACCOUNT]

9 Section 328.
10
11 (a) There is created the Wyoming generational investment
12 account. The account shall consist of all funds transferred or
13 appropriated to the account by law. All monies deposited or
14 transferred into the account are intended to be inviolate and
15 constitute a permanent or perpetual trust fund, subject to the
16 provisions for the withdrawal of funds provided by this section.
17
18 (b) The state treasurer shall invest all funds within the
19 Wyoming generational investment account in accordance with W.S.
20 9-4-715(a) and (c) through (e) and 9-4-716. W.S. 9-4-716(e) shall
21 not apply to the account. The state treasurer, or the treasurer's
22 designee, which shall be registered under the federal Investment
23 Advisor's Act of 1940, as amended and if required to be registered
24 by the terms of that act, may invest the unobligated, unencumbered
25 balance of the account in equities, including stocks of
26 corporations. The account shall be invested so that the overall
27 risk profile of the account's investments shall not materially
28 exceed the risk profile of a reference portfolio that consists of
29 eighty-five percent (85%) private equity and fifteen percent (15%)
30 private credit investments. In adopting investment policy
31 statements for the account, the state loan and investment board
32 shall seek to preserve the balance of the account in a manner that
33 strives for the highest possible risk-adjusted total net return
34 consistent with an appropriate level of safety and liquidity,
35 giving consideration to the account's potential extended
1

1 investment period and the extended period during which monies in
2 the account may be ineligible for withdrawal.
