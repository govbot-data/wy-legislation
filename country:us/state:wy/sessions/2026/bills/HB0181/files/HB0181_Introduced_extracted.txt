HOUSE BILL NO. HB0181
Biometric data and license plate readers-regulation.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Singh, Andrew and Provenza
and Senator(s) Ide, Pearson and Rothfuss
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to the administration of the government;
2 prohibiting governmental entities from using biometric data
3 to identify a person or verify a person's identity;
4 prohibiting governmental entities from using geolocation
5 data to identify the location of a person or motor vehicle;
6 prohibiting governmental entities from using automatic
7 license plate reader systems to identify motor vehicles or
8 collect fees on highways; prohibiting governmental entities
9 from contracting with third party vendors to use biometric
10 data, geolocation data and automatic license plate reader
11 systems; requiring governmental entities to adopt policies
12 as specified; providing exceptions; providing a civil cause
13 of action; amending the Wyoming Governmental Claims Act;
14 providing definitions; making conforming amendments;
