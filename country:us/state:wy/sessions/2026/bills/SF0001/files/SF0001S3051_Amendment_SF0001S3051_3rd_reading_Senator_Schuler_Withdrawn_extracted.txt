"[K-12 SUICIDE PREVENTION PROGRAMMING]
3
4 (a) There is appropriated fifteen million dollars
5 ($15,000,000.00) from the public school foundation program account
6 to the department of education-school finance to establish a school
7 district grant program for school years 2026-2027 and 2027-2028,
8 to augment amounts within the education resource block grant model
9 available to districts to provide suicide prevention education to
10 the K-12 student population as provided in this section. Amounts
11 allocated to each district shall be based upon the district's
12 average daily membership utilized in the education resource block
13 grant model for each school year in accordance with W.S. 21-13-
14 309(m)(iv).
15
16 (b) Each district receiving a grant under this section shall
17 report to the department of education the expenditure of amounts
18 awarded under this section, the number of students that received
19 services as a result of the grant and the impact of the services
20 provided by the district.
21
22 (c) The department of education shall promulgate rules
23 necessary to carry out this section.
24
25 (d) It is the intent of the legislature that the appropriation
26 in this section not be included in the department of education-
27 school finance's standard budget for the immediately succeeding
28 fiscal biennium.
29
30 (e) This section is effective immediately.".
