21 town demonstrates all of the following:

23 (i) The city or town does not currently have a digital
24 permit system or process available;
25
26 (ii) The adoption of a digital permit system will create
27 a standard permitting process and improve transparency;
28
29 (iii) The adoption of a digital permit system will allow
30 applicants to submit applications directly to the city or town for
31 permits.
32
