2 21-16-2001. Faculty research projects; incentives.
3
4 (a) The university faculty research project account
5 is created. All investment earnings from funds in the
6 account shall be credited to the account. Notwithstanding
7 9-2-1008, 9-2-1012(e) or 9-4-207, funds within the account
8 shall not lapse or revert. Funds within the account are
9 continuously appropriated to the university and shall
10 remain available for distribution as provided by this
11 section.
12
13 (b) The funds within the account created by
14 subsection (a) of this section shall be used to incentivize
15 and fund faculty research projects at the University of
16 Wyoming by providing funds for:
17
18 (i) Research projects where the acquisition of
19 enhanced research capabilities, including but not limited
20 to search engine and research database access, and
21 additional research-related equipment is necessary;
