2 40-32-101. Definitions.
3
4 (a) As used in this chapter:
5
6 (i) "DC fast charging station" means a device
7 used to charge a motor vehicle that meets the definition of
8 "DC Level 1," "DC Level 2" or "DC Level 3" as defined in
9 Standard J1772 of the SAE International standards and any
10 future updates to those standards, or an equivalent power
11 output level;
12
13 (ii) "Energy resource category" means coal,
14 natural gas, wind, solar, hydroelectric, nuclear,
15 petroleum, biomass or biogas, geothermal or any other
16 energy resource;
17
18 (iii) "Public utility" means as defined by W.S.
19 37-1-101(a)(vi).
