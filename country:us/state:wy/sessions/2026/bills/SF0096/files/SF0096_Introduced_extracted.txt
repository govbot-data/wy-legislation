6 39-18-106. Licensing; permits.
7
8 (a) Every wholesaler, including a remote retail
9 seller, and every cigarette importer and cigarette
10 manufacturer who sells or offers to sell nicotine products
11 in this state must have a license to do so issued by the
12 department. No license or renewal of a license shall be
13 granted under this section unless the wholesaler states in
14 writing, under penalty for false swearing, that he shall
15 comply fully with W.S. 9-4-1201 through 9-4-1209. The
16 license fee is ten dollars ($10.00) per year or fraction
17 thereof and is valid through June 30 in each year. The
18 license will be granted only to wholesalers who own or
19 operate the place from which sales are made and additional
20 licenses must be obtained for each separate location. The
21 licenses are transferable pursuant to rules and regulations
22 promulgated by the department.
