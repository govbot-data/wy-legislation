3 (a) The legislature finds that:

5 (i) Public confidence in election outcomes

9 (ii) The use of electronic tabulation and

13
14 (iii) Manual tabulation and counting of paper
15 ballots under uniform statewide standards will increase
16 election accuracy, transparency and voter trust;
17
18 (iv) Wyoming elections should rely on processes
19 that can be observed, audited, and replicated by citizens;

1 (v) It is necessary to repeal statutory
2 authorizations for electronic tabulation and e-pollbooks
3 and to establish statewide standards for manual tabulation
4 and counting that maintain flexibility for improved
5 counting methods over time.
6
7 (b) The purpose of this act is to transition all
8 Wyoming elections to paper ballots that are manually
9 counted in public view under uniform statewide standards
10 ensuring purity of the vote, transparency, security,
