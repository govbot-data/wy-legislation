3 35-2-1501. Short title; purpose.
4
5 (a) This act shall be known and may be cited as the
6 "Hospital Price Transparency Act."
7
8 (b) The purpose of this act is to require hospitals
9 to disclose prices for certain items and services and to
10 provide civil penalties.

4 35-2-1504. Monitoring.
5
6 (a) The department may monitor each facility's
7 compliance with the requirements of this act using any of
8 the following methods:

10 (i) Evaluating complaints made by persons to the
11 department regarding noncompliance with the act;
12
13 (ii) Reviewing any analysis prepared regarding
14 noncompliance with this act;

16 (iii) Auditing the websites of facilities for
17 compliance with this act.

19 (b) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the
20 contrary, in considering an application for renewal of a
21 facility's license or certificate, the department may
22 consider whether a facility is or has been in substantial
23 compliance with this act.
