2 (a) There is created the forest health grant program.
3 The grant program shall be administered by the state

8 (b) Except as otherwise provided in subsection (c) of
9 this section, grants under the forest health grant program
10 shall pay for not more than seventy-five percent (75%) of
11 the cost of an eligible forest health project as specified
12 in this subsection. Any other source of funds, cash or
13 in-kind contributions may be used as matching funds to
14 cover the remainder of a project's costs and the state
15 forester may require that matching funds be obligated to
16 pay for a portion of any project. Forest health projects
17 eligible for grant funding under this section shall be
18 conducted on state, federal government, local government or
19 private land, shall be necessary in the public interest and
20 shall be designed to accomplish one (1) or more of the
21 following:

23 (i) Enhancement of water yield or quality;

10 (e) There is created the forest health grant program
11 account. The account shall consist of those funds
12 designated to the account by law. Funds in the account
13 shall be used for grants authorized to be made under this
14 section. Any person may also grant, give, transfer, bequest
15 or donate funds to the account. Funds in the account shall
16 be invested as provided by law. Any funds remaining in the
17 account at the end of any fiscal year shall not lapse or
18 revert as provided by W.S. 9-4-207 but shall remain in the
19 account and be used to implement the purposes of this
20 section. Accrued interest and other earnings on unexpended
21 funds within the forest health grant program account shall
22 be credited to the account.
