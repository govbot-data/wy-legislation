Continuity of Permitting Act.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Filer, Fornstrom, Geringer,

Love and Pappas
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to cities and towns; requiring jurisdiction
2 over pending building permit or land use applications on
3 land annexed by a city or town to remain with the county
4 until the applications are issued or denied by the county
5 following annexation; requiring jurisdiction to transfer to
6 an annexing city or town as specified; prohibiting counties
7 from arbitrarily and capriciously denying applications for
8 the purpose of transferring jurisdiction; specifying
9 applicability; and providing for an effective date.

4 (c) Jurisdiction over a building permit or land use
5 application pending at the time that the subject land or
6 building was annexed by a governing body of a city or town
7 under this section shall remain with the county with whom

10 denies the final building permit or land use application,
11 at which time jurisdiction shall transfer to the annexing
12 city or town. No county shall arbitrarily or capriciously
13 deny a pending building permit or land use application
14 under this subsection for the sole purpose of transferring
15 jurisdiction to the annexing city or town.
