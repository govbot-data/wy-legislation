16 Section 1. That the Legislature of the state of Wyoming
17 hereby applies to Congress, under the provisions of Article
18 V of the United States constitution, for the calling of a
19 convention of the several states limited to proposing
20 amendments to the United States constitution that impose
21 fiscal restraints on the federal government, limit the power
22 and jurisdiction of the federal government and limit the terms
23 of office for federal officials and members of Congress.

3
4 (a) That the Legislature of the state of Wyoming adopts
5 the application made under section 1 of this joint resolution
6 expressly subject to the following reservations,
7 understandings and declarations:
8
9 (i) An application to the United States
10 constitution to call a convention of the several states
11 pursuant to article V of the United States constitution for
12 the purpose of proposing amendments to the constitution
13 confers no power to Congress other than the power to call the
14 convention. The power of Congress to exercise this
15 ministerial duty consists solely of the authority to name a
16 reasonable time and place for the initial meeting of a
17 convention;
18
19 (ii) Congress shall call an amendment convention
20 of the states only upon the receipt of applications for an
21 amendment convention for the substantially same purpose as
22 this application from two-thirds (2/3) of the legislatures of
23 the several states;

2 (iii) Congress does not have the power or
3 authority to determine any rules for the governing of an
4 amendment convention of the several states called pursuant to
5 article V of the United States constitution. Congress does
6 not have the power to set the number of delegates to be sent
7 by any state to a convention, nor does it have the power to
8 name delegates to a convention. The power to name delegates
9 remains exclusively within the authority of the legislatures
10 of the several states;
11
12 (iv) By definition, a convention of the states to
13 propose amendments to the United States constitution means
14 that states shall vote on the basis of one (1) state, one (1)
15 vote;
16
17 (v) A convention of the several states to propose
18 amendments to the constitution convened pursuant to this
19 application shall be limited to consideration of the topics
20 specified in this joint resolution and no other. This
21 application is made with the express understanding that an
22 amendment that in any way seeks to amend, modify or repeal
