5 35-2-1501. Short title; purpose.
6
7 (a) This act shall be known and may be cited as the
8 "Hospital Price Transparency Act."
9
10 (b) The purpose of this act is to require hospitals
11 to disclose prices for certain items and services, to
12 provide civil penalties and to prohibit debt collection by
13 hospitals in violation of this act.
