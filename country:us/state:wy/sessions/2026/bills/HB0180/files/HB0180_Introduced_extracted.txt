6 Section 3. There is appropriated five thousand
7 dollars ($5,000.00) from the general fund to the office of
8 state lands and investments for distribution to any city or
9 town that established a surface water diversion or runoff
10 system under W.S. 15-7-101(a)(iii) or 15-7-401 before the
11 effective date of this act that is seeking to transition
12 the system to a surface water drainage utility. The office
13 of state lands and investments shall distribute this
14 appropriation to cities and towns on a pro rata basis. This
15 appropriation shall be for the period beginning with the
16 effective date of this act and ending June 30, 2028. This
17 appropriation shall not be transferred or expended for any
18 other purpose and any unexpended, unobligated funds
19 remaining from this appropriation shall revert as provided
20 by law on June 30, 2028.
