1 Article 7, Section 1. Legislature to provide for public
2 schools.
3
4 (a) The legislature shall provide for the establishment
5 and maintenance of a complete and uniform system of public
6 instruction, embracing free elementary schools of every
7 needed kind and grade, a university with such technical and
8 professional departments as the public good may require and
9 the means of the state allow, and such other institutions as

11
12 (b) Notwithstanding any other provision of this
13 constitution, for the operating costs of the public schools,
14 the legislature shall provide a formula for funding, and that
15 formula shall be subject to judicial review for
16 constitutional compliance. The legislature shall fund the
17 formula by appropriation and the amount of this appropriation
18 shall not be subject to judicial review. In the event the
19 appropriation is insufficient to fund the spending provided
20 by the formula for funding, the spending shall be reduced by
21 a uniform percentage.
