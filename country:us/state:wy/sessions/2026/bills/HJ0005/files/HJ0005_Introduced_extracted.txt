Law Enforcement Memorial-Michael A. Rosa.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Washut, Harshman, Larson, JT

A JOINT RESOLUTION
for
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION encouraging the Wyoming Peace Officer
2 Memorial Committee to enshrine the name of Patrolman Michael
3 A. Rosa on the Wyoming Law Enforcement Fallen Officer
4 Memorial.
5
6 WHEREAS, Patrolman Michael A. Rosa, at the time of his death,
7 was twenty-nine (29) years old, was married and was the father
8 of two (2) daughters and a son; and
9
10 WHEREAS, Patrolman Rosa was a veteran of the United States
11 Marine Corps and served in the Vietnam War and with the Rock
12 Springs Police Department for eight (8) months as an
13 undercover narcotics officer prior to his death; and

1 WHEREAS, on Saturday, July 15, 1978, at approximately 1:00
2 a.m., Patrolman Rosa was shot and killed while seated in the
3 backseat of an unmarked police vehicle; and
4
5 WHEREAS, the case was diligently and thoroughly investigated
6 by the Wyoming Division of Criminal Investigation, and the
7 report was reviewed by the Sweetwater County prosecutor and
8 the person believed responsible was charged with murder; and
9
10 WHEREAS, after a fifteen (15) day long preliminary hearing,
11 a justice of the peace in Sweetwater County determined that
12 there was probable cause to believe that Patrolman Rosa was
13 murdered by this individual and ordered that the first degree
14 murder case move forward to trial in the district court; and
15
16 WHEREAS, the charged individual was represented at both the
17 preliminary hearing and the trial by a renowned trial lawyer
18 in the state and he was ultimately found not guilty of the
19 crime; and
20
21 WHEREAS, Patrolman Rosa's name has never been included on the
22 Wyoming Law Enforcement Fallen Officer Memorial at the

2 WHEREAS, the Officer Down Memorial Page is a nonprofit

1 Section 1. That the Wyoming Legislature encourages the
2 Wyoming Law Enforcement Memorial Committee to rectify the
3 omission of Patrolman Michael A. Rosa's name from the Wyoming
4 Law Enforcement Fallen Officer Memorial, and on or before the
5 next Wyoming Law Enforcement Fallen Officer Memorial service,
6 appropriately enshrine the name of Patrolman Michael A. Rosa
7 of the Rock Springs Police Department on the Wyoming Law
8 Enforcement Fallen Officer Memorial at the Wyoming Law
9 Enforcement Academy at Douglas, Wyoming.
10
11 Section 2. That the Secretary of State of Wyoming
12 transmit copies of this resolution to the Governor, the
13 Attorney General, the director of the Wyoming Law Enforcement
14 Academy and the Wyoming Peace Officer Memorial Committee.
