9 21-3-402. Wyoming language and literacy program;
10 purpose.
11
12 (a) The Wyoming language and literacy program is
13 created.
14
15 (b) The purpose of this act is to ensure that every
16 student in kindergarten through grade twelve (12) develops
17 strong language and literacy skills by establishing a
18 comprehensive system of evidence based language and
19 literacy instruction, assessment, intervention and
20 professional development that supports educators, engages
21 families and promotes literacy proficiency for all Wyoming
22 students.
