State parks-nonmotorized recreational trail fees.

for
1 AN ACT relating to state parks and cultural resources;
2 authorizing the department of state parks and cultural
3 resources to establish nonmotorized recreational trail
4 fees; providing for administration of the fees; creating an
5 account; providing for continuous appropriation of the

7 dates.

7 (d) There is created the nonmotorized recreational
8 trails account. Funds received from the sale or issuance of
9 permits under this section shall be deposited into the
10 account. The department may accept gifts, grants, donations
11 or other contributions for deposit into the account. Funds
12 deposited into this account are continuously appropriated
13 to the department of state parks and cultural resources for
14 the administration of the nonmotorized recreational trail
15 program. It is the intent of the legislature that funds in
16 the account shall supplement any funds appropriated to the
17 department of state parks and cultural resources for the
18 purposes and uses specified in this subsection. The funds
19 shall be expended only for:

21 (i) Maintenance, construction, signage and
