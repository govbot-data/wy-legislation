AN ACT relating to game and fish; allowing a person to use
a leashed blood-trailing dog to track and recover a killed
or wounded black bear; and providing for an effective date.
Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
Section 1. W.S. 23-3-109(d)(intro) is amended to
read:
23-3-109. Use of dogs; dogs injuring big or trophy
game animals may be killed; citation of owners of dogs
harassing game animals; penalties; leashed dogs for
tracking.
(d) A person may use one (1) leashed blood-trailing
dog to track a wounded or killed big game animal or black
bear within seventy-two (72) hours of shooting the animal.
