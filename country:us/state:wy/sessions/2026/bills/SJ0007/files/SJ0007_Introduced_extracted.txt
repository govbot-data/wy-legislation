4 The Wyoming Constitution provides each competent adult with
5 the right to make health care decisions. The adoption of this
6 amendment would provide that, notwithstanding any other
7 provision of this Constitution, only the legislature, not the
8 courts, may define what constitutes "health care" for the
9 purposes of this right. This amendment would also provide
10 that legislation regulating health care shall be subject to
11 judicial review under the "rational basis" standard of
12 review.
