5 (a) There is created the Wyoming business council.
6 The council is a body corporate operating as a state
7 instrumentality operated solely for the public benefit. As
8 such it shall have, and is hereby vested with, the powers
9 and duties conferred in this chapter. Beginning March 1,
10 2022, it shall be governed by a board of directors
11 consisting of thirteen (13) voting directors, appointed by
12 the governor with the advice and consent of the senate. The
13 governor shall be a member and cochairman of the board, but
14 shall not vote. The appointed directors shall have
15 demonstrated leadership and business expertise. An equal
16 number of directors shall be appointed to initial terms of
17 one (1), two (2) and three (3) years. Thereafter, directors
18 shall serve for terms of three (3) years. No appointed
19 director shall serve more than two (2) successive three (3)
20 year terms. If a vacancy occurs, the governor shall appoint
21 a successor in accordance with W.S. 28-12-101. The governor
22 may remove any board member he appoints for cause and shall
23 remove any director who fails to attend three (3)
