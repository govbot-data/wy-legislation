26 35-6-141. Right to have and provide an abortion.
27
28 (a) The state shall not deny or interfere with a person's

31 (i) Prior to the viability of the fetus; or

46
2

1 (a) Unless otherwise authorized by this act, any person who
2 provides medical treatment, provides medication or performs a

7 (b) Except as provided in subsection (a) of this section,

19 The good faith judgment of a physician, physician assistant,

28 (a) Any regulation promulgated by the state relating to
