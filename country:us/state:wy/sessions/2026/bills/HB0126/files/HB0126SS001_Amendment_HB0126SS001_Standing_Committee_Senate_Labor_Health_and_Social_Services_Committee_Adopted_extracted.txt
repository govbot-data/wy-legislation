23 35-6-502. No abortion after viability; exception.
24
25 An abortion shall not be performed after the embryo or fetus has
26 reached viability, except when necessary to preserve the woman
27 from an imminent peril that substantially endangers her life or
28 health, according to appropriate medical judgment.

30 35-6-503. Viability not affected by abortion.
31
32 A physician who performs an abortion procedure employed pursuant
33 to W.S. 35-6-502 shall not intentionally terminate the viability
34 of the unborn infant prior to, during or following the
35 procedure.
36
37 35-6-504. Means of treatment for viable abortion.
38
39 The commonly accepted means of care that would be rendered to
40 any other infant born alive shall be employed in the treatment
41 of any viable infant who survives an abortion or attempted
42 abortion. Any physician performing an abortion shall take
43 medically appropriate and reasonable steps to preserve the life
44 and health of an infant born alive.
