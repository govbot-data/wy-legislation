4 40-32-102. Virtual currency kiosks authorized;
5 regulation; penalty.
6
7 (a) No person shall own, operate or manage a virtual
8 currency kiosk in this state unless the person:
9
10 (i) Has been issued a license under the Wyoming
11 Money Transmitters Act, W.S. 40-22-101 through 40-22-129;
12 or
13
14 (ii) Is a financial institution and has been
15 granted a charter under title 13 of the Wyoming statutes.
16
17 (b) Any person who knowingly violates subsection (a)
18 of this section is guilty of a misdemeanor punishable by
19 imprisonment for not less than three (3) years, a fine of
20 not less than ten thousand dollars ($10,000.00), or both.
