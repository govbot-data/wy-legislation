A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to motor vehicles; requiring operators of
2 commercial motor vehicles to demonstrate proficiency in the
3 English language; prohibiting operators of commercial motor
4 vehicles who cannot demonstrate English language
5 proficiency from operating commercial motor vehicles as
6 specified; providing criminal penalties; providing
7 definitions; making conforming amendments; and providing
8 for an effective date.
9
10 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
11
12 Section 1. W.S. 31-18-608 is created to read:
13
14 31-18-608. English language proficiency for operators
15 of commercial vehicles; penalties.
1 HB0032

1
2 (a) As used in this section, "proficiency in the
3 English language" means an operator of a commercial vehicle
4 can demonstrate proficiency of the English language in
5 accordance with 49 C.F.R. § 391.11(b)(2).
6
7 (b) No operator of a commercial vehicle shall operate
8 a commercial vehicle within this state if the operator is
9 unable to demonstrate proficiency in the English language.
10 Any operator of a commercial vehicle who violates this
11 subsection shall be fined one thousand dollars ($1,000.00)
12 and shall be prohibited from operating a commercial vehicle
13 within this state until the operator is able to demonstrate
14 proficiency in the English language.
15
16 (c) Any operator of a commercial vehicle who operates
17 a commercial vehicle within this state while under the
18 prohibition imposed by subsection (b) of this section
19 shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a misdemeanor and
20 shall be punished by an additional fine of one thousand
21 dollars ($1,000.00), by imprisonment for not more than
22 ninety (90) days, or both.
